About Deborah Imaji

🎀 get to know more about me, my background, and my skills

As a dedicated 3D artist, I have honed my skills to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant pieces that blend the beauty of art and the innovation of technology. My art style, characterized by a cozy and inviting aesthetic, seeks to transport viewers into warm, comforting environments that evoke a sense of nostalgia and wonder.

With each project, I strive to capture the delicate balance between the tactile charm of traditional artistry and the limitless possibilities offered by cutting-edge digital tools.

Deborah Imaji

I am a 3d artist, a student, a frontend developer and an entrepreneur. Ever since I was a young girl, I have always had a passion for art and a passion for technology. Over time, I started learning different skills and techniques in these fields.

Eventually, I discovered an exciting way to combine my love for art and technology: 3d Art. I soon realised that with 3d modelling software such as Blender and Maya, I could create cozy art. Now, I want to share my art with you.
